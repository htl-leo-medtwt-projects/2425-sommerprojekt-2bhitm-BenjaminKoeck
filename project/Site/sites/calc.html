How to use the calculator

1. Select a commodity from the dropdown menu.

2. Choose the origin and destination locations. Purchase and sell price will be automatically filled.

OR Enter the purchase and sell prices.

3. Select your ship. The quantity field will be automatically filled with the ship's total cargo capacity.

OR Enter the quantity yourself.

4. Enter any additional expenses (e.g. refueling cost) and flight time. (optional)

5. The calculator will automatically update the outputs.
